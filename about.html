About us

PERSUIT is a family project conceptualized as a way to sustainably marry vintage materials with modern silhouettes. Modified menswear in beautiful vintage materials with high waistlines, wide legs and generous pockets is universally flattering while maintaining a stylish, easy irreverence.

All of our trousers are made to order and our raw materials are hand selected. We only source the highest quality vintage made from natural materials -- wool, cotton and silk. Once you have provided your measurements we will work with you to identify the right shape and material before lovingly tailoring them. Each item is one of a kind.

While the fashion industry at large produces an exorbitant amount of waste and 26 billion pounds of clothing end up in landfill annually, PERSUIT trousers give high quality vintage pieces new life.
