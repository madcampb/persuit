About us

Persuit at its core is a proof of concept of clothing and fashion’s inextricable link to environmentalism and sustainability. The harmful effects of the fashion industry are apparent, and despite good intentions over three quarters of clothing donations still end up in landfill. However, we believe it is possible to create a new, high quality item of clothing by using existing textiles that will otherwise get discarded.

While some fast fashion brands are making efforts to operate more "sustainably," volume-based business models simply cannot become sustainable. Only around ten percent of brands are implementing recycling strategies for their items and participating in the circular economy. Persuit demonstrates the capability to create contemporary pieces without adding to already overwhelming stocks.

Our brand reimagines high quality, vintage men’s trousers for women while maintaining the functionality, comfort, and timelessness of their original design. Each piece is carefully curated and tailored to ensure natural materials and classic shapes. We hope these beautiful pants will provide an easy, flattering, and stylish clothing option for women in professional and creative spaces, while solving some of the problem of immense waste which we see in the fashion industry today.
