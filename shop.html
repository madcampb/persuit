Reworked vintage trouser

Our trousers are lovingly reworked for women from the finest vintage materials. The inventory is hand selected from charity shops in a process which diverts high quality textiles from landfill while also supporting philanthropic organizations. We place a heavy emphasis on natural materials -- wool, cotton, silk, and cashmere -- which are nonpolluting post production.

We hope that these trousers will make you feel powerfully at ease. By co-opting masculine silhouettes and reimagining them, modern women can enjoy high waists, wide legs and deep pockets. These trousers are universally flattering and are tailored to suit the female form.

Wear your trousers with a vintage t-shirt for a casual day around town, a bodysuit for an evening look or a cashmere sweater for the office.

Please understand that each piece is pre-loved and may have slight imperfections from its previous life. To maintain the shape of your reworked vintage trouser, we suggest that you dry clean only.

The model is 5'7, size 28 and wears the pants in grey check.
